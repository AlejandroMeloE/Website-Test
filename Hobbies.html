Video Games

One of our main hobbies are playing video games. It's fun, can be sociable, provides challenge, and some of the stories can be amazing.

Some of these games are included however not limited to

The Dark Souls Franchise

The Dark Souls Franchise is a masterpiece that provides a magnificent storyline in a bleak world where everything is stagnant and where the main character seems like a spectator in the lives of all the other undead, yet is still chosen one to either rekindle the first flame or choose to cause it to sputter out.

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate

The newly released Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is a huge hit on the new console known as the switch, and provides fun to the casual players who want to have a good time and has a high enough skill ceiling for all the people that want to be the best in competitive. It also brings back a story mode that while not providing much story, instead providings challenges that range from trivial to frustrating, as well as uninteresting to fantastic.
